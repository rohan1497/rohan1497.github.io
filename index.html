What is NRAC ?

NRAC is a scientific gathering, where students interested in Robotics, can come and interact with professionals who have already excelled in this field and also gain some experience.

Our Goal

NRAC 2017 aims in proclaim knowledge and share new ideas amongst the professionals, industrialists and students from research area of Robotics to share their research experiences and indulge in interactive discussions at the event.

Why to Attend ?

With members from around the nation focused on learning about robotics and Automation technologies, this is your single best opportunity to reach the largest assemblage of participants from the Robotics and Automation community. Conduct demonstrations, distribute information, acquire knowledge about current and trending robotic and Automation technologies, make a splash with a new research, and receive name recognition at this 1-day event.

Who can Attend ?

NRAC is open to all. Whether you are a school student or an adult interested in Robotics. Be prepared to learn and have an amazing experience.
